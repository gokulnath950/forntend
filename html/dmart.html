About us.!

Avenue Supermarts Limited, d/b/a DMart, is an Indian retail corporation that operates
a chain of supermarkets in India. It was founded by Radhakishan Damani in 2002 when
its first store was opened in Powai, Mumbai. As of 14 December 2023, it has 341 stores
across 14 states in India

The full form of DMart is Damani Mart. DMart is a popular retail chain in India known
for its affordable prices and wide range of products
