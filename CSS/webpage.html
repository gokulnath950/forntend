As an exclusive North Indian & Chinese Restaurant, Gokul Veg was started in 1995, at DVG Road, Gandhi Bazaar. Gokul Veg went on to the top position in its rating for the quality of food. The same consistency and standard are maintained even today in all our units. South Indian delicacies became a part of the menu in many of our units. Gokul Veg today is branched out in all the corners of the Bangalore the Silicon City. Our branches are located at: YELAHANKA, RT NAGAR, GANDHI BAZAR, PADMANABHANAGAR.


We offer range of verities from South Indian and North Indian food. We can assure you with the best quality of food, timely delivery and most competitive rates. We maintain a hygienic and well-equipped kitchen housing well-trained Managers, Chefs and Staffs who will execute the food order in timely manner. We assure you that, as always, the food prepared will be of utmost quality & safe. We also ensure that all the food shall be prepared in hygienic manner with good quality ingredients and with fresh vegetables and fruits only.
